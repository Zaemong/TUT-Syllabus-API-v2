授業概要
／ 		Integrated English Iの内容をレベルアップし、より実践的な英語力の向上を目的とする。Integrated English Iと同様に、学生の興味・関心や進路に即した内容を学習することを通じて英語の4技能のスキルアップや国際的教養、ビジネス英語のさらなる修得をめざす。

In this course, students continue developing integrated linguistic skills using intermediate-level English materials. The course builds on the cultural and interdisciplinary foundations established in the first semester. The focus expands to culture through films, short videos, social practices, and discussions on current global issues. Students develop their vocabulary and strengthen their communication skills and cultural understanding by reading, listening, speaking, and writing, and conducting basic research activities.

The course continues TOEIC preparation toward a score of 400–500 and uses the second half of the designated textbook.

授業方法
／ 		Classes will be organized around textbook units, together with review sessions, applied activities, and progress checks. Students will check their answers on the Vocabulary Check Sheet they have prepared in advance, confirm the meaning of vocabulary, and answer listening practice questions.

The course continues to emphasize active learning through group work, including discussions, case studies, and collaborative research activities. Short movies and film-based materials are incorporated to support listening comprehension, cultural analysis, and critical thinking. Greater focus is placed on problem-based learning and analytical tasks that require students to apply language skills in cultural contexts. When necessary, laptops may be used for guided in-class activities. Feedback on quizzes, written work, and presentations will be provided in class or through Moodle.
履修上の注意
／ 		Please be sure to bring your textbook with you as you will be solving problems in each class. Also, bring a dictionary to look up the meanings of any words you don't know.
準備学習
／ 		A Vocabulary Check Sheet will be given as a preparatory assignment. Students must solve the problems before class (approximately 30 minutes). Students must complete and submit the review assignments in the assigned online workbook (approximately 30 minutes). Before classes that include progress checks, students must review all Vocabulary Check Sheets that fall within the scope of the check.
成績評価方法・基準
／ 		「授業参加姿勢 (30%)」「課題点 (30％)」「到達度チェック (40％)」を総合的に評価する。合格点は60％とする。

授業参加姿勢とは、積極的な発言、質問、グループワークやペアワーク等授業内活動に取り組む姿勢や予習・復習状況確認のために実施する小テストの結果に関する評価を含む。
