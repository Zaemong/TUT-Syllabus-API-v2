授業概要
／ 		１年次の学習を総合して、より高度な英語の運用能力の向上をめざす。英語ネイティブスピーカーによるCommunication skillsの向上、学際的的な内容を扱うAcademic English、グローバル時代の国際理解に必要な文化と社会に関する知識の修得をめざすCultural Studies、TOEICやビジネス英語扱うBusiness/TOEICなどを通じて、学生の興味・関心や進路に即したより高度な英語力の修得をめざす。


This course develops language skills and critical thinking through cultural knowledge with an interdisciplinary approach. It focuses on cultural diversity, global communication, shared human experiences, and topics such as cultural heritage, food, music, dance, and historical places.

The course is designed for students aiming for a TOEIC score of approximately 400–500. It is offered over two semesters, and students are encouraged to continue in the second semester.

授業方法
／ 		Classes are organized around textbook units, together with review sessions, applied activities, and regular assessments. Students will prepare Vocabulary Check Sheets in advance and use them in class to review answers, confirm word meanings, and work on listening practice.

This course emphasizes active learning through frequent group work and discussions, including pair tasks, and collaborative research-based activities. Feedback on quizzes and assignments will be provided during class and via Moodle. When necessary, laptops may be used for guided in-class work.
履修上の注意
／ 		Please be sure to bring your textbook with you as you will be solving problems in each class. Also, bring a dictionary to look up the meanings of any words you don't know.
準備学習
／ 		A Vocabulary Check Sheet will be given as a preparatory assignment. Students must solve the problems before class (approximately 30 minutes). Students must complete and submit the review assignments in the assigned online workbook (approximately 30 minutes). Before classes that include progress checks, students must review all Vocabulary Check Sheets that fall within the scope of the check.
成績評価方法・基準
／ 		「授業参加姿勢 (30%)」「課題点 (30％)」「到達度チェック (40％)」を総合的に評価する。合格点は60％とする。

授業参加姿勢とは、積極的な発言、質問、グループワークやペアワーク等授業内活動に取り組む姿勢や予習・復習状況確認のために実施する小テストの結果に関する評価を含む。
